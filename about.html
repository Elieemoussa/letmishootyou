A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Biography

I am a New Zealand-born photographer, currently working in New York City.

Known for portraiture, editorial and commercial work, I regularly contribute to various print publications throughout North America, Europe and Australia, as well as online.


ABOUT MY PROCESS

My Work

With a subtle minimalistic approach, I strive to create work that is clear and uncompromising.

I’m the recipient of multiple International Magazine Awards in both Portrait and Editorial photography, as well as the winner of the 2018 Miller Prize for Portrait Photography.
